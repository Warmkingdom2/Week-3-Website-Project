Title: Chibi School Boy

This is one of the earliest drawings I made. This was done at school during free time.
The reason I'm showing this one is to show that your work can change depending on how long you develop your skills.

Chibi is the style of drawing. It's originally a style of Japan "Anime" or "Manga" which are detailed humoid drawings of people and characters. Chibi is a short cute style of that style.

I've not done this style of drawing for 10 years however I still like the cute style it gives off.
